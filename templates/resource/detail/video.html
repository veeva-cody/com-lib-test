Navigating the New Medical Affairs Landscape: Data, Impact, and the Evolving MSL Role

This template uses this Podcast as example: https://www.veeva.com/resources/navigating-the-new-medical-affairs-landscape-data-impact-and-the-evolving-msl-role/

As the clinical landscape evolves, healthcare professionals are constantly navigating more complex data, and putting a greater demand on medical affairs for more scientific exchange.

The increasing visibility not only puts additional pressure on medical teams to be operationally effective but also to better demonstrate outcomes.

In this episode of Veeva’s podcast series, “What Pharma Needs Next”, Christoph Bug, VP Global Medical at Veeva, speaks to several industry experts about the ongoing transformation of medical affairs to become a more strategic, data-driven decision-making organization.
