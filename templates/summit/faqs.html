Frequently Asked Questions

Veeva Summit is for Veeva customers and other life sciences professionals only.

Veeva Commercial Summit will be in Boston, Massachusetts at the Boston Convention & Exhibition Center (BCEC).

Veeva Summit is an in-person event in Boston, designed for you to connect with life sciences professionals and experts from across the industry.

Veeva Commercial Summit is complimentary for Veeva customers and guests from life sciences companies.

You can register online until May 6, 2024. After May 6, you can register directly on-site at Summit at the Boston Convention & Exhibition Center (BCEC) during Summit hours. Please see the agenda for specific times.

You can email the Veeva Summit team at summit@veeva.com.

We have secured discounted hotel room rates for Veeva Summit attendees at various nearby properties. Use the Veeva booking link in your reservation confirmation email to book a room at your desired property.

Requests for accessibility accommodations and services can be made through the Summit registration form. Please indicate your requirements and a representative will contact you to discuss your needs to make sure your experience at the event is enjoyable. For any questions or to request more information regarding accessibility accommodations or services, please email summit@veeva.com.
